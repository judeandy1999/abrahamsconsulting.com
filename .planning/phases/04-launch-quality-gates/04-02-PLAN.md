## STRIDE Threat Register

| Threat ID | Category | Component | Disposition | Mitigation Plan |

| T-04-04 | Spoofing | JSON-LD injection | mitigate | Serialize typed objects with `JSON.stringify` on server-built data; no raw HTML injection of untrusted input. |
| T-04-SC | Tampering | npm/pip/cargo installs | accept | No new dependencies required for JSON-LD in this plan. |
</threat_model>
